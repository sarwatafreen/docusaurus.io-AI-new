## What is Robot Simulation?
Robot simulation is a virtual environment that mimics the real world to test and develop robots without physical limitations. It allows:
- Safe testing of new algorithms
- Rapid prototyping without hardware costs
- Reproducible experiments
- Training for real-world deployment

## Why Simulation Matters
Before robots enter the real world, they must be thoroughly tested in simulation. Major robotics companies like Boston Dynamics and Tesla use simulation extensively to train their robots. Understanding simulation is key to:
- Reducing development time
- Improving robot safety
- Testing edge cases
- Training AI models with synthetic data
